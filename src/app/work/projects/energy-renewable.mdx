## Overview

Energy is a comprehensive website for a renewable energy solutions provider, showcasing their expertise in solar and wind energy projects. Built with Next.js 14 and modern web technologies, this site demonstrates advanced frontend development with a focus on performance and user experience.

## Key Features

- **Solar & Wind Energy Solutions**: Comprehensive service showcase
- **Fully Responsive Design**: Seamless experience across all devices
- **Next.js 14 Performance**: Ultra-fast page loads and optimal Core Web Vitals
- **Modern Professional UI**: Clean, trustworthy design for enterprise clients
- **SEO Optimized**: Properly structured for maximum search visibility
- **WCAG Accessibility Compliant**: Inclusive design for all users
- **Blog & Project Showcase**: Content management for case studies and news
- **Lead Generation Forms**: Integrated contact and inquiry systems

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 14
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Deployment**: Cloud Platform
- **CMS**: MDX for blog content

## Design Approach

The design emphasizes trust, professionalism, and environmental consciousness. Clean lines, green accent colors, and high-quality imagery create a modern, corporate feel appropriate for B2B renewable energy services.

## Performance Optimization

- Image optimization with Next.js Image component
- Code splitting and lazy loading
- Server-side rendering for critical content
- CDN delivery for global performance
- Lighthouse score: 95+

## Impact

This website serves as a professional digital presence for a renewable energy company, effectively communicating their services and values while generating quality leads.

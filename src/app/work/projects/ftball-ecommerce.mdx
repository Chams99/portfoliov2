## Overview

FTBall is a professional e-commerce platform specializing in authentic football jerseys, banners, and equipment. The platform features official team merchandise from top European leagues including La Liga, Premier League, Ligue 1, and Serie A.

Built with modern web technologies, FTBall delivers a seamless shopping experience with a comprehensive product catalog, advanced filtering, and a modern French-language interface.

## Key Features

### Official Team Merchandise
- Authentic jerseys from top European clubs
- Official league merchandise
- Quality guaranteed products
- Licensed team equipment

### E-commerce Features
- **Comprehensive Product Catalog**: Browse hundreds of products
- **Advanced Category Filtering**: Find exactly what you need
- **Featured Products & Promotions**: Special deals and highlights
- **Shopping Cart System**: Easy checkout process

### Customer Benefits
- **Free Delivery**: On orders over €75
- **30-Day Returns**: Easy return policy
- **7/7 Customer Support**: Always available to help
- **Quality Guarantee**: Authentic products only

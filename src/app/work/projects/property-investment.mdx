<ProjectContent>
  <ProjectSection title="Overview">
    Built to bridge the gap between investors and premium real estate opportunities, this platform provides a curated experience for discovering high-yield properties in key UK markets like Manchester, Birmingham, and Leeds. The application focuses on trust, clarity, and conversion, presenting complex financial data in an accessible, visually appealing format.
  </ProjectSection>

  <ProjectSection title="Key Features">
    - **Interactive ROI Calculator**: Real-time financial projections allowing investors to estimate returns based on initial capital and property appreciation.
    - **Curated Property Portfolio**: rich media showcases for residential and student accommodation developments.
    - **Lead Generation Engine**: Optimized conversion funnels with downloadable investment guides and whitepapers.
    - **Responsive Design**: A mobile-first approach ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, from desktop to mobile.
    - **Location Intelligence**: Detailed area guides and market analysis for featured investment zones.
  </ProjectSection>

  <ProjectSection title="Lead Generation Forms">
    The platform employs sophisticated lead capture mechanisms to ensure high conversion rates.
    
    ### Consultation Booking
    A streamlined modal interface allows potential investors to book free consultations directly. The form captures essential details and investment goals to tailor the consultation experience.
    
    ![Consultation Booking Form](/images/projects/property-consultation.png)
    
    ### Investment Guide Access
    Gated content strategies are used to deliver value in exchange for contact information, building a qualified lead database.
  </ProjectSection>

  <ProjectSection title="Tech Stack">
    - **Frontend Core**: Semantic HTML5, Modern CSS3 with Custom Properties (Variables)
    - **Scripting**: ES6+ JavaScript for interactive calculators and DOM manipulation
    - **Design System**: Custom responsive grid layout with flexbox and glassmorphism elements
    - **Performance**: Optimized asset delivery and lazy loading for fast initial paint
  </ProjectSection>

## Overview

A property investment platform with ROI calculator, property listings, and lead generation forms. Built with HTML/CSS/JavaScript, featuring professional design and interactive investment tools.

## Key Features

- Property listings with investment details
- Interactive ROI calculator
- Lead generation and contact forms
- Investment guides and resources
- Mobile-responsive design
- Professional business styling

## Technical Implementation

### Frontend Architecture
- **HTML5**: Semantic markup and form structures
- **CSS3**: Professional styling with flexbox/grid
- **JavaScript**: Interactive calculator and form handling
- **Performance**: Optimized images and assets

### Interactive Features
- ROI calculation with real-time updates
- Form validation and submission handling
- Property filtering and search functionality
- Mobile-optimized touch interactions

### Business Features
- Lead capture and contact forms
- Investment calculator with multiple scenarios
- Property showcase with detailed information
- Professional design for investor confidence

## Development Challenges

- **Calculator Logic**: Implementing complex ROI calculations with multiple variables
- **Form Handling**: Creating professional lead capture forms
- **Performance**: Optimizing for business users on various devices
- **Design**: Balancing professional appearance with user-friendly interface

The design emphasizes luxury, trust, and professionalism. Every element is crafted to inspire confidence in potential investors, from the elegant typography to the sophisticated color palette.

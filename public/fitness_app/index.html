Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitLite

How accurate is the AI fitness advisor?

Our AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on millions of fitness data points. It provides personalized recommendations based on your goals, current fitness level, and progress, with accuracy rates above 95%.

Can I use FitLite without an internet connection?

Yes! FitLite works offline for basic features like step counting and workout logging. AI recommendations and food scanning require an internet connection for optimal performance.

What devices are supported?

FitLite is available on iOS and Android devices. We support all modern smartphones and tablets running iOS 12+ or Android 8+.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data with third parties. Your fitness journey is private and secure.
